Our Mission

In line with the government agenda of sustainable Agriculture we are offering an extensive range of locally sourced produce and support small scale farmers by providing market for their produce, giving them fair pricing to encourage produce sustainability.

About Us

Welcome to GreenWave Organic- Your online organic home delivery platform, we are very committed to the organic food movement, we practice agroecology to improve food yields for balanced nutrition consistently without damaging the environment. Our organic food is sourced from our farms and the local network of certified farmers. We pay the farmers a fair price for their sustainably grown food, and largely support them by providing market. We have the most flexible ordering and delivery options available to our customers. You can select from our vast range of fresh fruit & vegetables, fresh meat, groceries and even organic farm inputs for your Kitchen Garden!
